My Dog Coco
0:00 / 0:00

I have a dog named Coco — and despite the name, he's actually a boy! He's incredibly timid and clingy, and honestly, he can't stand being alone.

On a typical workday, he's by himself for over ten hours. I have a pet camera set up at home, so I check in on him now and then — and most of the time, he's just sound asleep, which is a relief.

But the moment I start getting ready to go out, he goes absolutely berserk. It's like he just loses it.

One time, I was watching through the camera and he completely disappeared from the frame — I panned around everywhere and couldn't find him anywhere. My heart nearly stopped!

I worry about him all the time. Even at work, I can't always focus knowing he's home alone. But I guess that's just what it means to love a dog this much.

I have a dog named Coco — / and despite the name, / he's actually a boy!
ドコという名前の犬を飼っています / でも名前に反して / 実は男の子なんです！
He's incredibly timid and clingy, / and honestly, / he can't stand being alone.
本当に臆病で甘えん坊で / 正直言って / 一人でいることに耐えられないんです。
On a typical workday, / he's by himself / for over ten hours.
普通の仕事の日は / 一人で / 10時間以上います。
I have a pet camera set up at home, / so I check in on him now and then — / and most of the time, / he's just sound asleep, which is a relief.
家にペットカメラを設置しているので / 時々様子を見るんですが / たいていは / ぐっすり寝ていてほっとします。
But the moment I start getting ready to go out, / he goes absolutely berserk.
でも私が出かける支度を始めた瞬間 / 完全に狂ったように吠えるんです。

ある時 / カメラで見ていたら / 完全に画面から消えてしまって / あちこちカメラを動かしたけど / どこにもいなくて。
My heart nearly stopped! / I worry about him all the time. / Even at work, / I can't always focus / knowing he's home alone.
心臓が止まりそうになりました！ / いつも心配しています。 / 仕事中でも / 集中できないことが多くて / 彼が一人でいると思うと。
