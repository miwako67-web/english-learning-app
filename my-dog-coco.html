My Dog Coco

重要単語（タップで解説）
I have a dog named Coco — and despite the name, he's actually a boy! He's incredibly timid and clingy, and honestly, he can't stand being alone. On a typical workday, he's by himself for over ten hours. I have a pet camera set up at home, so I check in on him now and then — and most of the time, he's just sound asleep, which is a relief. But the moment I start getting ready to go out, he goes absolutely berserk. It's like he just loses it. One time, I was watching through the camera and he completely disappeared from the frame — I panned around everywhere and couldn't find him anywhere. My heart nearly stopped! I worry about him all the time. Even at work, I can't always focus knowing he's home alone. But I guess that's just what it means to love a dog this much.

goes absolutely berserk
フレーズ
完全に狂ったように（吠える・暴れる）
✅ 感情の爆発を生き生きと表現。"barks a lot" より圧倒的に臨場感が出る
❌ "barks very very much" → 強調の重ね方が不自然
✓
can't stand being alone
フレーズ
一人でいることに耐えられない
✅ シンプルかつ自然。感情をストレートに伝える定番表現
❌ "doesn't like to be alone so much" → 回りくどい
✓
my heart nearly stopped
フレーズ
心臓が止まりそうになった（驚き・焦り）
✅ ドラマチックだがネイティブが日常的に使う慣用表現
❌ "I was very surprised" → 正しいが感情が弱く伝わる
✓
check in on him

✓
sound asleep

✓
