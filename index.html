Must-Visit Places in Japan
0:00 / 0:00

So, if you're ever planning a trip to Japan, you've gotta check out Kyoto and Osaka — they're totally different vibes, but both amazing in their own way.

Kyoto is all about the scenery and the beautiful traditional buildings. And if I had to pick just one spot, I'd say Kinkaku-ji — the Golden Pavilion — is absolutely unmissable. I mean, the way the gold just shines, and it reflects perfectly off the pond, with this gorgeous garden all around it… it feels almost dreamlike, like you've stepped into another world.

And here's my tip — go in the early morning. There's this soft, misty atmosphere that makes the whole place feel even more magical. It's honestly breathtaking.

Then, when you head over to Osaka, shift gears completely — because it's all about the food. The street food scene there is on another level.

So yeah — Kyoto for the beauty, Osaka for the food. That combo alone makes Japan so worth the trip.
